Account Deletion Request

If you wish to delete your account from Xodimlar kuzatuvi, please follow the steps below. This will result in the deletion of your personal data associated with the app.

Steps to Request Data Deletion:

  1. Send an email to softoptions.mobile@gmail.com with the subject line "Account Deletion Request".
  2. Include your first name, last name, and username in the email.
  3. Specify that you wish to delete your account and all associated data.

Data Deletion Details:

Upon receiving your request, we will delete the following data associated with your account:

  • First Name
  • Last Name
  • Middle Name
  • Photo
  • Phone Number
  • Username
  • Password

Please note that the deletion process is irreversible.

For further inquiries, contact us at softoptions.mobile@gmail.com
