Privacy and Policy for Xodimlar kuzatuvi

Introduction

Welcome to "Xodimlar kuzatuvi", developed by uzenergoinspeksiya. Our app is designed to track the location of authenticated employees at regular intervals, ensuring efficiency and safety in the workplace. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your data.

Permissions Used

  • Location Access: We use ACCESS_COARSE_LOCATION and ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION to determine your approximate and precise location.
  • Foreground Service: The FOREGROUND_SERVICE permission allows the app to run a service in the foreground.
  • Background Location Access: With ACCESS_BACKGROUND_LOCATION, the app continues to track location even when not in active use.
  • Battery Optimizations: REQUEST_IGNORE_BATTERY_OPTIMIZATIONS helps the app function without being hindered by battery optimization processes.
  • Internet: The INTERNET permission is necessary for transmitting location data back to our servers.
  • Notifications: POST_NOTIFICATIONS and RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED are used to send and manage notifications related to the app.
  • Device Wake: WAKE_LOCK allows the app to prevent the phone from sleeping to ensure continuous tracking.
  • Vibration Control: VIBRATE is used to control vibration for alerts and notifications.
  • Full Screen Intent and Alarms: USE_FULL_SCREEN_INTENT, SCHEDULE_EXACT_ALARM, and USE_EXACT_ALARM are used for alarm management and full-screen operations.
  • System Alerts and Notification Policy Access: SYSTEM_ALERT_WINDOW and ACCESS_NOTIFICATION_POLICY are required for specific system interactions and notification management.

Data Usage and Protection

We collect location data and related analytics to monitor employee movements during work hours. All data is securely stored on our servers, with access limited to authorized personnel. We do not share your personal data with third parties without your explicit consent, except as required by law. Robust security measures are in place to protect your data from unauthorized access or breaches.

User Rights

Users have the right to access their data and request corrections or deletions. Users can opt out of location tracking by changing the app’s permissions in their device settings. However, this may affect the app’s functionality.

Updates to the Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Users will be notified of significant changes through the app or via email.

Contact Information
